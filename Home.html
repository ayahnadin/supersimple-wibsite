Definition of plants Plants are autotrophic organisms that can perform photosynthesis. They can get their food from chlorophyll, sunlight, carbon dioxide and water, and through this chemical process they produce oxygen and glucose. Plants are of great importance in our lives, as they are an essential component of the environment.
