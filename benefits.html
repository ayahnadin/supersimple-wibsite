Benefits of Plants
  1. The ultimate benefit of plants is the air we breathe.
  2. Plants also provide other ecosystem services, like cleaning outdoor and indoor air.
  3. Plants also have other positive impacts on our health.
  4. Plants can also help reduce crime.
  5. Another ecosystem service plants provide is their ability to clean up contaminated soils.
