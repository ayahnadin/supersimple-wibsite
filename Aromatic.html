Aromatic plants
(1)
Name: Lavender
Family: Lamiaceae family of the Lamiaceae order
images:
Origins of the plant: The islands of the Atlantic Ocean, and it is now spread in all regions of the Mediterranean basin, Sudan, Somalia, and the Arabian Peninsula.
Where it is found: This plant is native to the Mediterranean, where it grows in warm, sunny climates. In cold climates, it may require additional protection in winter or be brought indoors during frost.
